Your files, always one click away.

teenyshelf sits in your menu bar with a badge showing how many files are on the shelf. Click to open, and your parked files appear in a clean grid - each with its thumbnail, filename, and size. Drag files out to any app or folder. You can also drop files directly onto the menu bar icon without opening the shelf first. The popover stays open when you switch apps, so you can navigate to Finder and drag back without losing your place.

Keyboard shortcuts for everything.

Press Option+Shift+S to open or close the shelf from any app. Use Option+Shift+D for Quick Drop. It grabs the selected files in Finder and adds them to the shelf instantly. No dragging required. Both shortcuts are fully customizable in Settings.

References, Not Copies

TeenyShelf stores URL references - never copies your files. A 10 GB video shelves as fast as a text file with zero extra disk space.

Files on external drives or network volumes are tracked gracefully. Disconnect a drive and items dim; reconnect and they restore automatically.

Drop the same file twice and it is quietly skipped - no duplicates on your shelf, ever.
